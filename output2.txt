# Defining Artificial Intelligence in 2025

**Date:** 2025-08-26

**Problem Statement:** Artificial intelligence (AI) is a rapidly evolving field with diverse interpretations.  This research aims to provide a 
clear and concise definition of AI, encompassing its current capabilities and limitations.

**Key Questions:**
- What is the current, widely accepted definition of Artificial Intelligence?
- How do different subfields of AI (e.g., machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing) contribute to the overall concept?     
- What are the ethical considerations and societal implications of defining and utilizing AI?

**Method Brief:**
- Review and synthesize existing literature on AI definitions from academic and industry sources.
- Analyze prominent AI systems and applications to identify common characteristics.
- Examine the evolution of AI definitions over time to understand shifts in understanding.

**Deliverables:**
- A concise, up-to-date definition of AI suitable for a broad audience.
- A summary report outlining the key characteristics and subfields of AI.
- An identification of key challenges and future research directions in defining AI.
